Munich
Bavaria, Germany
  • 🎫State museums — including the Pinakotheks and Deutsches Museum — are free every Sunday.
  • 🚂The Bayern-Ticket covers all regional trains in Bavaria for the day — perfect for cheap Alpine day trips.
  • 🌸Late May brings the Stadtgründungsfest near Marienplatz — free outdoor festival in the city centre.

Vienna
Austria
  • 🎫The Vienna Pass gives free entry to 60+ attractions — worth it if you plan to visit Schönbrunn and the Belvedere.
  • 🎻Late May overlaps with the Wiener Festwochen — a city-wide arts festival with many free outdoor events.
  • 🚋The Ringstraße trams pass every major landmark — a day ticket doubles as a free sightseeing tour.

🇦🇹
Salzburg
Austria
  • 🎫The Salzburg Card covers free entry to all major sights plus unlimited public transport — almost always worth it.
  • 🎶The Pfingstfestspiele classical music festival runs in late May inside the famous Festival Halls.
  • 🏔️The Old Town is very compact and walkable — save your transport budget for the Alpine day trips.

Lisbon
Portugal
  • 🎫The Viva Viagem 24hr pass (€6.80) covers all trams, funiculars, buses, and the metro — buy at any metro station on arrival.
  • 🎉Late May kicks off Santo António festival preparations — bunting, sardine grills on street corners, and a festive neighbourhood atmosphere.
  • 🌊The Atlantic coast is 45 minutes away by train — surf lessons, turquoise beaches, and Sintra all within easy day-trip reach.
Explore Lisbon guide →
🇵🇹
Algarve
Southern Portugal
  • 🌡️Late May is the sweet spot — 25°C, sea at 19–20°C, zero peak-season crowds, and long evenings with sunset after 9pm.
  • 🚂The regional train links Faro, Lagos, Tavira, and Silves cheaply — no car needed for the main coastal towns (€3–7 most journeys).
  • 🏖️Benagil Cave and Praia da Marinha rank among Europe's most beautiful coastal scenery — book boat tours ahead as they fill quickly in May.
